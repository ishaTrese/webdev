About Us

Threadful is a small, heart-driven store that offers handmade plushies, unique keychains, and custom bags. Our goal is to bring a little more warmth and creativity into your everyday life with affordable, cute, and functional items.

Each product is carefully crafted or selected with love. Whether you're gifting a friend or treating yourself, we're here to make your shopping experience delightful.

Thank you for supporting small and local!
